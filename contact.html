Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is the nearest railway station?

Mathura Railway Station is just 2.5 km away (approx. 10 mins drive). We can arrange pickup on prior request.

❓ Do you offer airport transfers?

Yes, we provide airport transfers from Agra Airport (KUA) at an additional charge. Please inform us 24 hours in advance.

❓ Is parking available?

Complimentary parking is available for all in-house guests with 24/7 security surveillance.

❓ What are the check-in/check-out timings?

Check-in: 12:00 PM | Check-out: 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out subject to availability.

❓ Can I host a wedding at the hotel?

Absolutely! Our 4500 sq.ft banquet hall and garden venue can host up to 600 guests. Call +91 90270 38209 for exclusive packages.
